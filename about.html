ABOUT US

The Tajniya Jewellers Beginning

The story of Tajniya Jewellers is no fairy tale but an epic scripted meticulously by six generations of the Tajniya clan. It all started way back in the 18th century, the group gets its name from the late Anna Rahul Tajniya , a doyen of the family and the chief architect who captured the hearts and imagination of the people with his ethics and his jewellery.

We Started In 1832, Expanding The Tajniya Trust.

With unbeatable trust gained at chain of stores in Pune, TJ Tajniya took Mumbai and other major cities of Maharashtra to enrich its market-hold. Today, the brand has exclusive stores in Mumbai, Aurangabad, Nagpur, Ahmednagar, Panvel, Goa and Nanded. Also spreading its global wings across US & UAE. The Tajniya online store is operated from Mumbai. While purity and transparency continue to earn the trust scores of customers, what also sets the brand apart is the wide variety comprising of Gold necklaces, bangles, vedhnis, chains, mangalsutras, ear-rings, rings etc. Through various intricately crafted collections, the brand has distinct styles to offer. The flagship Heritage Collection has jewellery in polki, jadau, uncut diamonds and traditional Maharashtrian jewellery. Some of their special Collections include Balgandharva, Temple, Royal and Wild Gold. Keeping the affordability quotient of customers too in mind, the brand has beautifully designed certified diamonds jewellery.

Reaching online audience with Tajniya majestic jewellery shopping experience

The Tajniya brand has started ecommerce store during 2012 with all its exclusive collection to cater nationwide audience with majestic online jewellery shopping experience. The E-store takes the online jewellery shopping experience to the next level with Excellent UI, access to extensive designs from elegant daily wears to royal Indian traditional designs, and wide price range.
